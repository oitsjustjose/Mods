Auto-Smelt


Auto-Smelt enchanted books are crafted in an anvil with a Lava Bucket and a Book & Quill

The smelting effect stacks with Fortune, and can be applied to any generic tool (from any mod). XP drops are calculated, and if a block for some reason does not apply the Fortune quantities to it, there is an override list in the config as well!

XP costs for the recipes are configurable
Hypermending


Hypermending enchanted books are crafted in an anvil with a Nether Star and a Book & Quill

Similar to normal Vanilla Mending, Hypermending repairs your tool - however, it does so rapidly (constantly in fact) and at no recurring cost - hence the upfront cost. Can be applied to any generic tool or armor.

XP costs for the recipes are configurable
Lumbering


Lumbering enchanted books are crafted in an anvil with a golden axe and a Book & Quill

This enchantment can be applied to any generic Axe, and allows you to cut down whole trees. This "treecapitator" effect only takes place while sneaking, and cuts down most of the tree; if it's a very wide tree that has many branches to the side, it may not get all of it. It will also destroy any nearby log blocks... so be careful!

XP costs for the recipes are configurable
Imperishable


Imperishable is an enchantment that can be obtained only in Nether Fortress Chests.

This enchantment can be applied to any tool, weapon or armor. It prevents said equipment from being used (and therefore damaged) when said equipment only has 1 durability left.
Multifaceted


Multifaceted enchanted books are crafted by throwing an undamaged Diamond Axe, Diamond Shovel, and Book & Quill in world. You "fuse" them together with the force from an in-world explosion (I suggest TNT).

This enchantment can only be applied to Pickaxes, and allows the pick to efficiently harvest any block!
Step Boost


Step boost enchanted books are crafted in an anvil with 16 stairs (of any mods') and a Book & Quill, and can also be acquired through random enchanting.

Allows the player to walk up one-block tall slopes without having to jump (can be walking in any direction).

XP costs for the recipes are configurable
Better Feather Falling


If you have a pair of boots with Feather Falling IV on them, all fall damage is negated.

The damage you would have taken (literally - the amount of half-hearts you would have taken) is then applied to directly to your boots instead.
