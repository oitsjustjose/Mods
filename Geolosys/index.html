Geolosys

Bringing Real Geological Systems to Minecraft

Geolosys is designed to a mod-compatible, customizable ore generation mod that likens back to TerraFirmaCraft.

Unlike TerraFirmaCraft, Geolosys is designed with compatability in mind. You can customize many components from which deposits generate, where they generate, what blocks are within the deposit and more. You can customize Geolosys to fit your pack as much as you'd like, adding in your own ores, removing ones you wouldn't want, and more. You can do this via the JSON config (for 1.12.x and 1.14.x) or via CraftTweaker (1.12.x only - as of now).

Getting Started in Geolosys

You'll want to get started with a field manual - by default it's crafted like what you see on the side. If you're on 1.14.x and it's enabled, you'll be gifted a field manual when you first join the world. This manual tells you everything you need to know, from the mechanics of Prospecting, deposit generation, and mod compatability to a detailed item-by-item list of each possible ore deposit and important specifications about it (such as minimum and maximum Y level, what block is within in, and more).

Want to Learn More for Modpack Dev?

If you want to read up on the capabilities of the Geolosys ore generation system and how to tap into its potential for modpack dev, go read the documentation on how to do so! As mentioned, CraftTweaker Documentation does not yet apply to 1.14.x.
