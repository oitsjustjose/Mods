Inspired by TerraFirmaCraft

Modded Minecraft makes mining all too easy - ther are dozens of ways of destroying all of the world in a quick way, but what about an interesting way?

That's where Geolosys steps in. Geolosys adds a Geolological system to Minecraft, with real-life mineral variants for all of the common ores you find.

Designed for Modpacks

With TerraFirmaCraft, the issue is that once you have finally found your ore and mined it (the fun part), processing it into an ingot isn't easy. And that's how it should be, for its overall design

As for Geolosys, the realistic mineral variant of ores is what generates, but when you break them you'll get something that is familiar, easy to use, and easily processed however you like.
For compatibility, you can even silk touch some ores to get their Vanilla variants!

Ore Samples & Variants
As the ores in Geolosys are rare, you'll find random samples scattered on the terrain's surface in of the type of ore contained in that chunk.
Here is a full list of minerals mapped to their appropriate resource:
  • Hematite: Iron
  • Malachite: Copper
  • Cassiterite: Tin
  • Galena: Silver & Lead
  • Platinum: Platinum
  • Cinnabar: Redstone
  • Limonite: Iron & Nickel
  • Azurite: Copper
  • Teallite: Tin
  • Bauxite: Aluminum
  • Autunite: Uranium
  • Assorted Quartz: Nether Quartz + Others (if available)
